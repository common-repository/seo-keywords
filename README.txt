== Description ==

SEO Keywords is a powerful plugin that helps you add keywords in your wordpress posts. Automate keywords building with ease! SEO Keywords automates keywords building in WordPress. It is a powerful plugin that integrates with Google and automatically adds keywords based on the suggestions directly from Google!

For make SEO Keywords works you have to create an account in wpseoplugins.org and generate an API KEY. This can be done easily in settings page of SEO Keywords. When API KEY is generated you have to perform a connection to Google Search Console (https://search.google.com), using Google Connect button in settings page or simply using Add Links button in SEO Keywords Metabox inside post page.

From settings page you have 2 methods for generate API KEY, login and registration. Both of them perform a call to api.wpseoplugins.org using email, password, name and surname. All of your data is treated according to our privacy policy (https://wpseoplugins.org/privacy-policy/) and our terms and conditions (https://wpseoplugins.org/terms-conditions/).

== Installation ==

Using the plugin is easy. Install and activate the plugin. Then signup to receive an API key from the SEO Keywords settings page. Then connect to your Google Search Console. If you did not verify your site, please follow the guidelines to Verify and add your site to Google Search Console here: https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9008080

Done. You can now go into any post or page, and automagically add keywords to your posts.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I use the plugin? =

Simply activate the plugin, go to plugin settings page on the left sidebar "SEO plugins" menu. create an account or login to your account. that's it!

now you can connect to Google Search Console in order to get information about your website. If you did not verify your Google account in order to receive data about your website, you can visit this guide to verify your site ownership to Google (https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9008080).

once the plugin is connected to your site and search console, simply click on "Get Keywords" inside each post or page, to automatically insert keywords in your content.

Every get keywords click has a cost of 1 credit. With your API KEY you got 10 credits for free. After you can buy credits in wpseoplugins.org with your account.

= What is Google Search Console =

It is a platform owned by Google that, once you've verified you are the owner of your website, will provide you with useful information on how your website interact with Google, what people sees after searching queries on Google and discovering your website.
